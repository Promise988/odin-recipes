Pesto

Description

Pesto is a vibrant green, fresh-tasting sauce that originated in Genoa, Italy. Itâ€™s traditionally made with a combination of fresh basil, Parmesan cheese, olive oil, garlic, and pine nuts.

Ingredients

  • Basil
  • Cheese
  • Oil
  • Nuts
  • Garlic
  • Lemon Juice

Steps

  1. Combine basil, Parmesan cheese, olive oil, pine nuts, and garlic in the bowl of a food processor or blender. Blend to a smooth paste. Add lemon juice, if desired, and quickly pulse to combine.
